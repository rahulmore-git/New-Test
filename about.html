About Pravin CHA wala

Your trusted digital logistics partner, revolutionizing the way businesses handle freight forwarding and supply chain management.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Pravin CHA wala emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to digitize and simplify the complex world of logistics and freight forwarding. What started as a small team's dream to eliminate the hassles of traditional shipping has grown into a comprehensive digital platform serving thousands of businesses worldwide.

Our journey began when our founder, Pravin, experienced firsthand the challenges of international trade - endless paperwork, hidden costs, poor communication, and lack of transparency. Determined to change this, we built a platform that puts power back in the hands of businesses.

Our Mission

To democratize logistics by making freight forwarding transparent, efficient, and accessible to businesses of all sizes. We believe that every company, regardless of its size, should have access to world-class logistics services without the traditional barriers.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading digital logistics platform, transforming how goods move across borders and making international trade seamless for everyone.
